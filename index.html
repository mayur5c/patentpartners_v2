Eligibility

  • Open to faculty, scientists, and adjuncts at recognized Indian higher-education institutions or national labs—no age limits [faculty-focused] [web:18][web:9]
  • Registered in India or in process; ready to convert to a Private Limited Company if selected [company readiness] [web:4][web:9]
  • Incorporated within the last 10 years for spinouts; pre-company faculty must show intent to incorporate within 90 days post term sheet [timeline pattern] [web:9][web:4]

How it works

  1. Phase 1: Call & screening

    Online intake with TRL self-check, brief deck, IP status, and TTO letter; initial panel shortlist [intake and screening flow] [web:18][web:3]

  2. Phase 2: Diligence

    Deep dives on IP, FTO prelim, regulatory, commercialization plan, and MVP roadmap [diligence emphasis] [web:9][web:4]

  3. Phase 3: Readiness sprints

    Mentorship and masterclasses on GTM, regulatory, and sales to refine pitches and milestones [masterclasses detail] [web:18][web:9]

  4. Phase 4: Demo Day

    Offline pitches at host campus to investor syndicates and corporate partners; decisions at panel discretion [demo day] [web:18][web:3]

  5. Phase 5: Incubation

    Fast-track admission, OKRs, and pilot facilitation; funds post-compliance and recognition [incubation follow-on] [web:18][web:9]

FAQs

Who can apply?

Faculty, scientists, and adjuncts affiliated with recognized Indian institutions or labs; early spinouts are also welcome—no age limits [eligibility orientation] [web:18][web:9]

Do applicants need to be registered entities?

Registration is optional at application; selected teams must be ready to incorporate as a Private Limited Company prior to equity disbursal [entity policy] [web:4][web:9]

Are pre-revenue and MVP stages eligible?

Yes—pre-revenue, MVP, or product–market fit stages are eligible, with rubrics weighted to research translation [stage policy] [web:4][web:18]

Are all sectors eligible?
